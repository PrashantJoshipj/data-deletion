Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how users can request deletion of their data from our application.

Option 1: If you would like to delete your data, please email us at youremail@domain.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Include any details (such as your user ID) that will help us locate your data.

Option 2: If you have a user dashboard, you can go to “Account Settings” → “Delete My Data” (if implemented).

Once we receive your request, we will delete your data from our records within 48 hours (or your stated timeframe) and confirm with you once the process is complete.
